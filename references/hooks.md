# Hook Design Reference

The hook determines ~80% of content performance. Invest disproportionate time here.

## 5 Proven Hook Formulas

### Formula 1: Identity + Secret
```
"我是[身份]，我要告诉你别人不会说的[话题]"
"I spent [time] as [role]. Here's what nobody tells you about [topic]."
```
**Why it works:** Insider access + exclusivity. Reader feels they're getting privileged information.

Examples:
- "I spent 10 years as a Netflix algorithm engineer. Here's how recommendations actually work."
- "我做了4年留学中介，这是行业不会告诉你的5件事"

### Formula 2: Number + Contrast
```
"从[A]到[B]，我只用了[时间]（没有[常见误解]）"
"How I went from [bad state] to [good state] in [time] (no [expected shortcut])"
```
**Why it works:** Specific numbers = credibility. Parenthetical denial removes skepticism preemptively.

Examples:
- "From $200K in debt to millionaire in 4 years (no crypto, no inheritance)"
- "从月薪3000到年入百万，我用了3年（没有家里帮忙）"

### Formula 3: Time Investment + Discovery
```
"我花了[时间]研究[话题]，这是我的发现"
"I spent [large number] hours studying [topic]. Here are [N] things that changed everything."
```
**Why it works:** Large time investment = you don't have to. Reader gets compressed value.

Examples:
- "I spent 500 hours studying top writers. These 6 frameworks changed my writing forever."
- "我花了3个月研究了100个爆款推文，总结出这5个规律"

### Formula 4: Anti-Consensus + Stance
```
"大多数人认为的[A]其实是错的"
"The most [superlative] advice about [topic] is actually [opposite]"
```
**Why it works:** Cognitive dissonance triggers curiosity. Reader must find out why they're wrong.

Examples:
- "The most underrated skill that changed my career: strategic laziness"
- "99%的人对紫微斗数的理解都是错的"

### Formula 5: Specific Group + Direct Command
```
"如果你是[人群]，读这个："
"If you're a [demographic], read this:"
```
**Why it works:** Exclusivity + direct address. Target reader feels "this is literally for me."

Examples:
- "If you're a 20-something male, read this:"
- "如果你是30岁还在迷茫的人，这条推文写给你"

## Hook Quality Tests

1. **3-Second Test**: Would you stop scrolling?
2. **Friend Test**: Read it to someone — do they say "然后呢?"
3. **24h Test**: Write it, sleep on it. Still compelling tomorrow?
4. **Specificity Test**: Does it have a number, name, or timeframe?
5. **Promise Test**: Is there a clear payoff for reading?

## Hook Anti-Patterns (avoid these)

- ❌ "在这个快速变化的时代..." (generic, zero information)
- ❌ "今天我想分享一些想法" (no hook, no promise)
- ❌ "关于X，有很多值得说的" (vague, no urgency)
- ❌ "Thread 🧵" alone (lazy, no value proposition)
- ❌ Starting with a definition ("根据维基百科...")
